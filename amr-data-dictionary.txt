AMR data (LAST UPDATED 18/09/2024)

100,000 rows of 6 variables

These data represent the sort of data that might be obtained from the Second Generation Surveillance System. There is one record for each specimen taken. 

id | Integer | A unique identifier for each record within the data
firstname | Character | The first name of the patient from whom the sample was taken
surname | Character | The surname of the patient from whom the sample was taken
specdate | Date | The date that the specimen was taken
dob | Date | The patient's date of birth
nhsnumber | Character | The patient's NHS number
organism | Character | The species name of the organism identified in the sample
rAMOXICLAV | Integer | Whether the sample is resistant to Co-Amoxiclav, 1 is resistant, 0 is not resistant
